Our Story

Born out of a whiteboard, a marker, and one woman's mission to make math simple, relatable, and joyful.

From Whiteboard to Digital Platform

School of Mathematics, Nigeria Ltd was founded by Rose Danjuma Uwanassara with a clear vision: to become Africa's leading destination for mathematics learning, resources, and innovation.

We don't just teach math - we teach confidence, build communities, and prepare learners for a tech-driven future where numeracy is power. From WAEC prep to global partnerships, we're transforming how mathematics is learned across Africa.

Our Mission

To help students understand and enjoy math through content, community, and creative learning.

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about our courses and platform

How do I register for the bootcamp?

Simply click the "Register via WhatsApp" button and send us a message. We'll guide you through the simple registration process.

Are scholarships really available?

Yes! We offer need-based scholarships. Contact us to discuss your situation and we'll work out a payment plan that works for you.

What if I miss a live session?

All sessions are recorded and shared with registered students. Plus, our WhatsApp support helps you catch up quickly.

Do you offer one-on-one tutoring?

Contact us to discuss personalized tutoring options. We can arrange individual sessions based on your specific needs and budget.
